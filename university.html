University of Waterloo

At the University of Waterloo a Bachelors of Computer Science program is avaliable. The admissions requirements are...

  • Ontario students: six Grade 12 U and/or M courses including...
  • Advanced Function
  • Calculus and Vectors
  • Any Grade 12 U English
  • One other 4U course

    • Grade 11 intro to Computer Science is recommended along with an average of high 80s

    First year program

    As a first year at waterloo, you can expect to take many math and computer science related courses. Some courses you may take include...

  • Algebra for Honours Mathematics
  • Calculus 1 for Honours Mathematics
  • Designing Functional Programs
  • One communication skills course
  • One elective

    • Following that, you make take...

  • Linear Algebra 1 for Honours Mathematics
  • Calculus 2 for Honours Mathematics
  • Elementary Algorithm Design and Data Abstraction
  • Elective or Probability
  • one elective

    • How to complete undergraduate degree

    The duration of the program is 4 years in order to get the diploma. Some requirements to achieve this include...

  • As a minimum, students must have a 60% overall average
  • Students must complete a minimum of 20.0 units
  • Students must complete two courses from list 1 (communication or english based courses) OR Complete one course from list 1 and one course from list 2 (also communication based courses)
  • An example from list 1 is : Oral Communications for Academic Purposes
  • An example from list 2 is : Communications in Mathematics and Computer Science
