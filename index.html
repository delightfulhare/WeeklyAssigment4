What does a Systems Analyst do?

As a Systems Analyst, they must design, develop, and test IT systems. This means that as a systems analyst must program computer systems, monitor how they function and locate any problems that may be found in the system. If there happens to be an error in the system, the systems analyst must come up with a solution.

Education requirements

Education requirements

If you would like to become a systems analyst, you must...

  • Have a bachelors in computer science or any related discipline
  • Have experience as a computer programmer
  • Have a professional certification for example, Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) or another related to IT
